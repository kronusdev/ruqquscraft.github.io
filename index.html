Server Rules

To maintain a friendly community and server experience for all, please follow the following rules.

  1. Be kind to your fellow player
  2. No griefing, no hacked clients
  3. No asking for OP or a staff position
  4. No spamming, No advertising of any kind
  5. No chunkloaders or redstone contraptions designed to cause lag
